Frequently Asked Questions

How often are new blog posts published?

We publish 2–3 blog posts each month, covering research highlights, student projects, and insights from the world of genetic engineering.

Do I need to subscribe to access the blog?

No — all blog posts are free to read. Subscribing to our mailing list ensures you never miss new content and exclusive behind-the-scenes updates.

Can I contribute to the blog?

Yes! We welcome contributions from students and researchers. Reach out to us if you’d like to share an article, project update, or opinion piece.

Who writes the blog posts?

Posts are written by our executive team, club members, and guest contributors from the wider UofT community.

What topics does the blog cover?

Our blog explores synthetic biology, genetics, club news, event recaps, and student experiences in research and learning.

Where can I find blog updates?

Updates are shared on Instagram, through our mailing list, and always appear on this website’s blog section.
